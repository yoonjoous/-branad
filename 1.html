Introduction to Brands

A brand is a product with unique character, for instance in design or image.
It is consistent and well recognized. The advantages of having a strong brand are:

• Inspires customer loyalty leading to repeat sales and word-of mouth recommendation • The brand owner can usually charge higer prices, especially if the brand is the market leader • Retailers or service sellers want to stock top selling brands. With limited shelf space it is more likely the top brands will be on the shelf than less well-known brands.
